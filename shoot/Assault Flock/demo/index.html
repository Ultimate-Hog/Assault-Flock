HOW TO PLAY

  1. NEST — Spend Seed to recruit birds into your Roster. Each bird has a species, role, and optional traits.
  2. ROSTER / FORMATION — Arrange your flock. Pick a formation that suits your strategy (Flying V for healing, Tight Cluster for aura stacking, etc.).
  3. CALL CARDS — Set conditional rules: "IF flock HP < 50% THEN switch to Evasive." Cards fire automatically — no mid-run input.
  4. CAMPAIGN — Your flock auto-fights through 3 stages. Each stage ends with a boss. Survive all 3 to complete a run.
  5. DEBRIEF — Earn Seed from kills & survivors. Spend it in the Nest to rebuild after losses.
  6. RELEASE — Return a bird for 80% Seed if you need funds. Use it strategically when veterans fall and you need fresh recruits.
  7. INFINITE MODE — After Boss 3, Stage 3 loops with ×1.25 threat scaling per loop. Seed rewards scale with loop count.
TIP No Seed and no Roster? A Feathered Loiterer will join for free from the Nest — weak enough to replace, strong enough to earn your next recruit.
